1. Open gesture_library_demo_v1.bpf
2. Give proper links to .brs and npm files referred in presentation
3. Publish the presentation to player
4. On iPhone or Android mobile open network settings and connect to sign net WiFi
5. Open browser instance in mobile and hit https://brightlink.app
6. Click on get permission button
7. On iOS it will ask for permission click on allow
8. Flick right/left, Flick pitch action of mobile will show relevant message on screen
9. /dwp/captiveportal.webmanifest is responsible for PWA feature that will allow app to open as App without address bar "app-like feel" on launching from home screen button

Tested using firmware 8.1.69
